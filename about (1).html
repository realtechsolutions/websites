About us

Manushree chemicals is a youthful organization setup in 2016 as a manufacturer of various chemicals, catering the customers nationally & internationally mainly fulfil the necessity of paper industry.
We manufacture products through strict quality norms testing in our laboratories to make them world class to fulfil the customers accurately.
Our strengths lie in our unique, proven technology & our experienced professional team, both of which have put the Indian company on a strong foundation in a short span of time. Manushree chemicals offers high quality Chemicals for Paper Industry.
We employ the most experience people with pulp and paper making experience, process understanding and chemical knowledge, that they could quickly understand your process, needs and then design programmes to solve your problems. This enhances runnability, quality and productivity & provides value to your mill.
